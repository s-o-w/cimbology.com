A Tale of Expertise

Meet Jack and Susan, two pillars of awesomeness at their electrical engineering firm.

Jack

A master substation physical designer with over 30 years of experience.

Jack creates the physical layouts and ensures that substations are properly designed for construction and maintenance.

With exceptional skills in AutoCAD and Autodesk Inventor, he's the go-to expert that colleagues rely on daily.

His meticulous approach to design has made him an invaluable asset to the Physical Design group.

Susan

An electrical engineer with 30+ years designing protection and control schemes for the substations her company designs and builds.

Susan knows AutoCAD Electrical like the back of her hand.

She always knows exactly where to find the right standards, specifications, and information needed for any project.

Her expertise and attention to detail has made her an indispensable resource to the entire team.

Their Legacy

Both Jack and Susan possess extensive knowledge of their craft. Over decades, they've built comprehensive libraries of files, documents, and data that they update and maintain like clockwork, on every project they work on.

They always know where to begin, where to look, and how to find information to answer questions for the many junior and mid-career engineers they mentor.

Jack and Susan are GREAT at what they do...

The Challenge

Jack and Susan are retiring next month.....

Although they've shared all their specifications, drawings, files, and documents with their team, it's an overwhelming amount of information.

How will the team digest this wealth of knowledge?

How will they use it effectively?

How can they find what they need when they need it?
